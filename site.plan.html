Overview

Purpose

Built a website for a small company that is dedicated to organizing social events, especially weddings in country places outside the city. This website will be informative about the services of the company.

Audience

This website is directed to couples who want to get married, people between 18 and 45 years of age interested in making their wedding a great memory and having a wedding planner to take care of the organization.

Content

Home page

Our advisory services start from the choice of the place, planning and supervising every detail that arises during the wedding... ending until the last minute to make this moment unforgettable.

You have an idea of ​​your wedding, bring it and we will develop it, if you don't know where to start, how to manage your guests, the organization and everything that goes into planning a wedding, we will guide you and together we will leave a mark on each guest. Don't worry, just think about the dress and the earrings, we'll take care of the magic.

Images for the Home page

STATES

Choose the right place according to the needs and preferences of the couple, we have several country estates that meet the expectations for a wonderful wedding.

Images for the Page 2

AMEVEA STATE

A spacious place surrounded by beautiful green areas and natural environments, where your wedding (Catholic, civil or Christian) will be wonderful, we have large parking and security areas, easy access by private car or taxi.

REMANSO STATE

It is a perfect place for your wedding, with large beautiful gardens and a chapel, we are close to Bogotá, 45 minutes away, easy access. The place to make your wedding a dream.

NOGALES STATE

We offer facilities that have large and comfortable spaces, ideal for celebrating your wedding from start to finish. You will find a beautiful chapel, where the ceremony can be carried out and a beautiful room for the reception, both specially designed for your wedding. You will also be able to enjoy the beautiful gardens that will serve as a setting for photographic recording.

DECORATION

Decoration is important to achieve a unique wedding adapted to the couple. We take care of every detail to create a calm, organized and detailed atmosphere, setting the place with natural flowers of the bride and groom's choice, tablecloths, napkins and plates base, according to the couple's profile.

Decoration identify you!! All details are important.
